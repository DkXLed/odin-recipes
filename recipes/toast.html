Plain Ol Toast

This is just basic toast, dont get too excited

Prep time=1 minute

Cook time=3 minutes

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Sugar and cinnamon optional

Directions

  1. Put bread in toaster
  2. Let toaster do it's magic
  3. Take toast out and butter it
  4. (Optional) add sugar and cinnamon if you're not a heartless beast
