The Best Steak Ever

This is one of the easiest, yet most satisfying steak recipes that will ever come across your grill!

Prep time=20 min

Cook time=13 min

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Powdered Garlic
  • Onion salt
  • Lawrys Seasoned Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Prepare steak by adding Lawrys Seasoned Salt, Garlic Powder, Onion Salt, and Pepper.
  2. While steak rests and settles closer to room temperature, start the grill and heat to 450 degrees F.
  3. Once grill is heated, place steak on the grill
  4. Heat each side for 4-5 minutes for medium rare (which is the only way you should eat steak)
  5. Remove steak from grill and let rest for 5 minutes.
  6. Enjoy the best steak of your life!
